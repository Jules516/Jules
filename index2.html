SCHOOL

I began my education at the Sacred Hearts of Jesus and Mary School in Cavite. I started at kinder on pre-school when I was 6 years old, then I enrolled in the same school for my elementary. On my entire pre-school and elementary I was just studying, School and home are the places I always go. Because of my shyness, I didn't interact that much, but I graduated with honors in elementary. I received average grades in high school because I lacked confidence in participating in extracurricular activities. My grades were insufficient to graduate with honors, but it became my motivation during my senior year of high school. I began to believe in myself and my abilities. I graduated with honors from high school and hope to graduate with honorary degrees from PUP.

HOME

My first residence was in Silang, Cavite. I lived there with my grandparents on my father's side, and my grandparents always looked after me because my parents were always working. As I mentioned in my education, I never play outside; instead, I play with my brother using our electronic devices. After I graduated from elementary school, we moved here in Carmona, Cavite, to an apartment with which I was unfamiliar in my first year, so when it came to school projects or activities, I always relied on my classmates. I honestly don't know my neighbors; I just see them outside and my parents are the only one who interacts with them. So, after I graduate, I plan to buy our own home and provide for my parents' needs.
